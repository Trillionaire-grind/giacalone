About the Neighborhood

A Legacy of Luxury

Port Royal is Naples' most coveted and exclusive neighborhood — a private peninsula bordered by Naples Bay, Gordon Pass, and the Gulf of Mexico. Home to some of the finest estates in all of Florida, Port Royal consistently commands the highest property values in the region, with residences regularly trading in the tens of millions.

Residents enjoy direct Gulf and bay access, the prestigious Port Royal Club, and a serene privacy that only true waterfront peninsula living can offer. Arpin & Giacalone have an unmatched record of luxury transactions within this sought-after enclave.
